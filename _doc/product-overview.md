# QuantumByte Landing Page

## What It Is
A simple, single-page branded landing page for QuantumByte. Two full-viewport sections showcasing the QuantumByte brand identity with animated visual effects.

## Sections
- **Section 1 — Hello QuantumByte**: Full-screen hero with large gradient text (purple → indigo) on a near-black background (#0a0a0f). Animated purple glow pulse behind the text. QuantumByte logo positioned top-center (placeholder at `/logo.png`).
- **Section 2 — We Love Quantum x QuantumByte**: Full-screen dark section (#0d0b1a) with bold heading and a field of 24+ scattered heart/love emojis (❤️ 💜 💙 🩷 💖) in mixed sizes and pink/purple/red tones. 10 floating hearts animate upward with opacity fade via CSS keyframes.

## Brand & Visual Style
- **Primary palette**: Deep dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f, #0d0b1a), purple-to-indigo gradient accents
- **Typography**: Bold, massive display type; white or purple-indigo gradient text
- **Tone**: Celebratory, warm, brand-forward, playful
- **Logo**: QuantumByte logo (to be provided by user; `/logo.png` placeholder with graceful hide on error)
- **Brand reference**: https://quantumbyte.ai

## Audience
General visitors and QuantumByte community members — first-time visitors discovering the QuantumByte brand.

## Purpose
Introductory brand landing page. A warm, celebratory first impression of the QuantumByte identity — no auth, no backend, no forms. Pure visual brand expression.

## Scope
Static single-page Remix route (`/`). No authentication, no database, no API. Two sections only.
